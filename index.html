About Me

Crafting digital experiences

I'm Silvy Rahman Urmi, a Fullstack Developer and Web Engineer currently pursuing my Master's degree at Technische Universität Chemnitz. My work lives at the intersection of web engineering, computer vision, and human-computer interaction.

From building Django-powered research platforms at Fraunhofer IVI to developing AI-driven interfaces that respond to eye movements and hand gestures, I love pushing the boundaries of what technology can do for people. Currently working as a Fullstack Developer at Koinon, TU Munich.

Beyond code, I'm a published researcher with work in machine learning and distributed systems — and a firm believer that the best software is the software that feels invisible.
